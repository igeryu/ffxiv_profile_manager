:: Files ::
Character - COMMON.DAT - Character Settings. (Seems to be modified at every login, regardless of which character is selected)
Character - CONTROL0.DAT - Keyboard/Mouse Settings
Character - CONTROL1.DAT - Gameplay Settings
Character - ITEMFDR.DAT - unknown
Character - ITEMODR.DAT - Inventory and Retainer item sort positions
Character - LOGFLTR.DAT - Chat filters
Character - UISAVE.DAT - timers such as the retainer venture timer, possibly other stuff

Gearset - GEARSET.DAT - Saved gearsets

HotbarLayout - HOTBAR.DAT - contents of your Hotbars, but not their positions or such
HotbarLayout - MACRO.DAT - Custom macros

Keybind - KEYBIND.DAT - Keybindings

UiLayout - ADDON.DAT - UI settings such as HUD layouts and window sizes.

Unused - ACQ.DAT - The list of recent tell contacts.
